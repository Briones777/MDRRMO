Our Guiding Principles

Mission

To protect lives, property, and the environment through effective disaster risk reduction, preparedness, response, and recovery programs, ensuring community resilience and safety at all times.

Vision

A disaster-resilient community with empowered citizens, capable institutions, and sustainable development practices that minimize risks and hazards.

Core Values

Integrity

We uphold honesty and transparency in all actions.

Service

We prioritize the safety and well-being of the community.

Preparedness

We promote readiness and proactive disaster management.

ResponseTeamwork

We collaborate with stakeholders for effective response.

Resilience

We strengthen the community’s ability to recover and adapt.

Accountability

We take responsibility for our actions and decisions.
